Tirupati Balaji Temple, Andhra Pradesh

The Tirumala Venkateswara Temple is one of the most visited and richest pilgrimage centers in the world. Dedicated to Lord Venkateswara (a form of Vishnu), it is located in the Tirupati hills of Andhra Pradesh and draws millions of devotees annually.

Kedarnath Temple, Uttarakhand

Kedarnath is one of the holiest Hindu shrines dedicated to Lord Shiva. Located in the Garhwal Himalayan range, the temple stands at an elevation of 3,583 m and is accessible only by trekking through the mountains or via helicopter.

Meenakshi Amman Temple, Tamil Nadu

Located in Madurai, this temple is dedicated to Goddess Meenakshi and Lord Shiva. It is a marvel of Dravidian architecture with its towering gopurams, vibrant sculptures, and centuries-old legacy.
